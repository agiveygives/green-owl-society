Primary Goals of the Green Owl Society

Introduction

The Green Owl Society (& Advice Club) was created to allow awesome people the opportunism to relax, imbibe, and diplomatically discuss anything that is on our minds. Period. No hidden agenda, no underlying plan or platform. We just like to have a cocktail and hear about what's going on in each other's world. And if solicited, give friendly advice -- But never would we judge our fellow Green Owls. You do you, Boo. We really don't care what you do.
